🖥️ Virtual Machines vs 🐳 Containers

The single most important idea in Day 1. Click “Add 3 apps” and watch why containers are so much lighter than virtual machines.

🏠 Analogy: A Virtual Machine is like building a separate house for every guest — each with its own foundation, plumbing, and kitchen (a full operating system). A Container is like giving each guest a private room in one shared apartment building — they get their own space, but share the building’s foundation and utilities (the host OS kernel). Same privacy, a fraction of the cost.

Why containers win for most workloads

Notice the repeated red Guest OS blocks on the VM side — every app drags a whole operating system with it. Containers share one OS kernel, so they’re smaller, start in seconds, and you can run far more of them on the same machine.
